# RGCS Non-Claims

A list of things this project does **not** claim, kept because the
subject matter attracts overstatement and because several of these
were claimed at some point and then withdrawn.

## Physical

- **Not** claimed: any measurement. The corpus contains zero records
  at any physical evidence class.
- **Not** claimed: metric actuation, spacetime engineering, gravity
  control, portals, nonlocal transport, or time travel.
- **Not** claimed: detection of Phryll or any new mechanism.
- **Not** claimed: spin memory in quartz.
- **Not** claimed: planetary grid structure. No geophysical data has
  been loaded.
- **Not** claimed: that any source-corpus frequency, angle or
  geometry has demonstrated significance.

## Mathematical and methodological

- **Not** claimed: novelty for the DDS common-closure formula. It is
  1/gcd(fᵢ) on the DDS grid and is published — Nicholas & Samueli
  (1987), Hwang et al. (2017), Fujifilm US12422666B2.
- **Not** claimed: novelty for the principle that a null needs a
  calibrated detector. That is the severity requirement
  (Mayo & Spanos) and the positive control, independently renamed in
  four fields.
- **Not** claimed: novelty for integer ambiguity resolution, wide-lane
  or dual-carrier thinning. Textbook GNSS.
- **Not** claimed: novelty for barycentric frame chains, emission
  coordinates, or relativistic clock models. IAU, SPICE, astropy,
  Coll–Ferrando–Morales, Rovelli, Ashby.
- **Not** claimed: novelty for multipath channel estimation. The
  programme's own novelty boundary declared it textbook in v4.7.
- **Not** claimed: that 4096 = 2¹² is a discovery. It is arithmetic.

## Validation

- **Not** claimed: validation against published *measurements*. An
  earlier version said the clock model was validated against
  Pound–Rebka and GPS. It was not: 2.45 × 10⁻¹⁵ is the theoretical
  prediction gh/c², so the comparison was of a formula against
  itself. The measured value was ≈ (2.57 ± 0.26) × 10⁻¹⁵. The claim
  is withdrawn; what is established is correct reproduction of
  standard formulae.
- **Not** claimed: that the analysis plan is externally
  preregistered. It is an `INTERNAL_ANALYSIS_FREEZE`; no third-party
  registry holds anything.
- **Not** claimed: that BOM prices or instrument figures are quotes.
  They are catalogue-class estimates.

## Integration

- **Not** claimed: that the integrated coordinate framework has
  demonstrated value. No case exists where it caught an error the
  separate standard tools would miss. That is the evaluation it
  needs and has not had.

## Standards and authority

- **Not** claimed: any standards authority. Protocol maturity is
  `EXPERIMENTAL_SCHEMA`, with one implementation, one author, no
  independent governance body, no adoption and no security review.
  Authority follows adoption; it is not asserted by authorship.

## Contact, timelines, and lore

- **Not** claimed: any relationship between the source corpus's
  narrative material and physical reality.
- **Not** claimed: any prediction, contact event, or timeline claim.
  Source narrative is archived as `SOURCE_CLAIM` or `LORE` under
  investigation-by-refusal, never as a finding.

## Why this file exists

Several entries above were once stated more strongly and were
corrected after adversarial review or prior-art search. Keeping the
withdrawals visible is the point — a project that only ever adds
claims is not being checked.
