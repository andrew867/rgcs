# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**
(https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in
our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, and to act and
interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse,
inclusive, and healthy community.

## Project-specific norms

Because this project sits at the intersection of conventional engineering
and unconventional source material, two norms get special emphasis:

1. **No ridicule in either direction.** The historical corpus and its
   authors are treated with respect (see the Historical & Source
   Companion); equally, insistence on controls, classification, and
   failure conditions is not "closed-mindedness" and is not up for
   relitigation in code review.
2. **Argue from registers, not from status.** Claims live in the claim
   register with classes and evidence; "I'm sure it works" and "everyone
   knows that's impossible" carry equal weight: none.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may
be reported to the maintainer at **me@andrewgreen.ca**. All complaints
will be reviewed promptly and fairly; the maintainer is obligated to
respect the privacy and security of reporters.

Enforcement follows the Contributor Covenant 2.1 community-impact
guidelines (correction → warning → temporary ban → permanent ban).
