# E07 — Operator state, meditation, breathing, blinded human protocols

Coverage: **H009–H016, F008–F011**.
Status: **`ETHICS_APPROVAL_REQUIRED`** — blocked, not performed.

## Order of operations is the design

This campaign may not run until **E06 (human loading)** and the
automated baseline exist. Without them, any "operator effect" is
indistinguishable from a warm hand pressing slightly harder during the
condition the operator finds more engaging.

The residual after mechanical and capacitive subtraction is
**predeclared** as the outcome. Not the raw shift. The residual.

## Conditions

- Automated, no-operator baseline.
- Standardized hand position and **force-matched** contact (a
  force-matched condition is the difference between a study and an
  anecdote).
- Breathing protocol.
- Neutral imagery vs target imagery.
- Meditation vs matched quiet rest.
- Expectancy manipulation only if ethically approved.
- Grounded / ungrounded / glove / no-contact controls.
- 40 / 40.96 / 41 / 42 Hz comparisons where safe.

## Measurement

**Objective crystal outputs only.** Optional EEG, heart rate,
respiration, hand capacitance as covariates. Within-subject randomized
crossover.

The dependent variable is a property of the crystal, never a report of
the experience. Subjective experience is real and is not evidence of an
external physical effect.

## Blinding

Condition labels are separated from analysis until lock
(`blind_labels`). The analyst does not know which trial was
"meditation". Expectancy is assessed, because a participant who expects
an effect grips differently.

## Boundaries (from the E07 prompt)

- **No unapproved human experiments. None have been run.**
- **No implication of consciousness transmission.**
- **Subjective experience is not confirmation of an external physical
  effect.** This is the boundary the entire T-lane quarantine exists to
  protect, and it applies here at the bench.
- No therapeutic or entrainment claim from 40 Hz.

## Ethics packet (prepared, not submitted)

Consent template, withdrawal procedure, data minimization, privacy
policy, adverse-event handling, randomization plan, and the
`ETHICS_APPROVAL_REQUIRED` gate wired into the protocol record.

## Blocker

**IRB/ethics determination — a human-only action.** No participant has
been recruited, consented, or measured, and the safety gate refuses to
issue a hardware-enable status regardless of how safe the engineering
is.
