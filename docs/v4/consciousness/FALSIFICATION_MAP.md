# T10 — Consciousness falsification map

Coverage: **C001–C052**. Gate: **G40**. Lane: **quarantined**.
Generated from `consciousness_lane.theory_registry`.

## How to read this

Every one of the 52 entries carries a layer, a status, an evidence
tag, and a **falsification condition**. This map is the index of what
would have to happen for each idea to be wrong.

An idea with no falsification condition is not preserved as science
here — it is preserved as metaphor or private myth, and labelled as
such.

evidence for it. `microtubule_threshold` is the clearest case — the
model exists precisely to show the hypothesis failing.

## The strongest surviving hypotheses

1. **C048 (coherence is not truth)** — not a hypothesis but a control,
   and the most useful thing in the lane.
2. **C032 (quantum cognition)** — has a parameter-free prediction that
   can fail. Still not a quantum brain.
3. **C010 (ring attractor)** — a real mechanism with real evidence in
   biology, though the "holographic" framing adds nothing.
4. **C024/C045 (synchrony)** — real, measured, and modest.

## The weakest, stated plainly

- **C016/C015 (microtubules)** — fails its own causal threshold by

- **C020/C022 (THz/superheterodyne)** — analogy only; water opacity
  alone likely defeats it.
- **C030/C042 (external field)** — `k_ext` is expected to fit to zero.
- **C031 (multiverse)** — outside empirical reach.

## Every null and negative condition

Nulls are first-class (G48). ORPHAN-016 preserves the corpus's own
recorded nulls. A `k_ext` that fits to zero, a gamma that dissociates
from report, a PAC that does not beat surrogates, a microtubule τ_c at
1e-13 s — **each is a result**, and each is registered rather than
discarded for being unexciting.
