# T06c — Aura translation

Coverage: **C029, C044**. Gate: **G36**. Lane: **quarantined**.

## The translation

"Aura" is translated into a **behavioural, attentional, emotional,
thermal, acoustic, electrical, or social influence radius** — each of
which is measurable, and none of which requires a new field.

**This is explicitly NOT an electromagnetic claim** (C029's registry
entry says so), and it is emphatically not a quantum one.

## What is genuinely measurable around a person

| Channel | Real magnitude | Detectable at |
|---|---|---|
| Thermal (IR) | body ~37 °C radiating | ~metres |
| Acoustic | breathing, movement, heartbeat | ~metres |
| Electrical | ECG ~mV at skin; cardiac field | ~cm |
| CO₂ / humidity | exhaled plume | ~0.5 m |
| Behavioural | posture, gaze, micro-expression | social distance |
| Attentional | others orient to a confident person | a room |

Every one of these is real, ordinary, and sufficient to explain the
phenomenology of "sensing someone's presence". A person entering a room
genuinely changes the room's temperature, sound, chemistry, and social
dynamics.

## The falsification

**Falsified if** no behavioural influence gradient with distance
survives sensory-cue controls. The design: vary distance, block each
ordinary channel in turn (visual, acoustic, thermal, olfactory), and
test whether any influence survives the blocking.

The historical record on this design (the Kirlian photography
literature, therapeutic-touch tests) is uniformly negative once the
ordinary channels are controlled. That is stated here as the prior, not
hidden.

## C044 — sixth/seventh-sense separation (`SOURCE_HYPOTHESIS`)

**Falsified if** discrimination performance is at chance in a
forced-choice design. Forced-choice is the right instrument because it
removes the response-bias explanation: a person who *feels* they can
tell either can or cannot pick the right box above chance.

## Why translate rather than delete

The experience of an "aura" is real as an experience. The ordinary
channels above are a full, honest, and *more interesting* account of it
than a field would be — and they can be measured. Deleting the term
would lose the phenomenon; endorsing the field would fabricate a
mechanism. Translation keeps both feet on the ground.

## Boundary

**Auras are not quantum fields.** Nothing here claims, implies, or
tests one.
