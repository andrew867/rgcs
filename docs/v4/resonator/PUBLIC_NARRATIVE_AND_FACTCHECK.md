The truthful narrative arc, with its fact-check ledger. Every scene's
claim resolves to an artifact; anything that cannot is marked LORE and
stays out of the evidence voice.

## The arc (truthful version)

1. **A feeling about crystals and frequencies** — presented as lore
   and personal history, in the first person, never as findings.
2. **The decision to test it properly** — the v2→v3 freeze
   discipline: pin everything, hash everything, preregister the
   claims (H-01..H-30), expect nulls.
3. **Building the instrument** — the validated FEM stack (the mass
   matrix bug and its fix is a GOOD scene: it shows the machinery
   catching errors), the capability firewall that refuses to fabricate
   answers.
4. **The anomaly that got smaller and stranger** — the Eye: found at
   4 mm resolution, threshold corrected (V4C-D-001), reclassified,
   refined, and finally understood as one apex feature with a
   symmetric family. The narrative arc IS the correction trail — the
   story is the method working, not a discovery being suppressed or
   confirmed.
5. **From drawings to a platform** — the closed-loop resonator
   platform: design-for-trim, guarded lasers that don't exist yet,
   certificates that refuse to overclaim.
6. **Where it stands** — everything computational, nothing measured;
   the claim card v4 as the honest summary; the open question stated
   as open.

## Rules

- **Lore is labelled lore** in the same breath it is told. The
  private ledger's consent rules (L01) govern which personal material
  may appear at all.
- No dramatic sentence detaches from its caveat: quotes come from the
  claim card, with version (V02 rule).
- Nulls and corrections get equal screen time to findings — in this
  programme they ARE the findings.
- No health, consciousness-effect, or anomalous-field framing,
  including by implication or by b-roll juxtaposition.
