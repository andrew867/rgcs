## Header: assumptions and conventions

1. Every row is classified under the four-category policy (see `SCIENTIFIC_CLASSIFICATION_POLICY.md`):
   **Established** (standard, independently verifiable mathematics/physics), **Derived** (computed within RGCS from stated inputs, reproducible), **Hypothesis** (RGCS conjecture requiring measurement), **Source claim** (statement by an external source — including the three peer-reviewed papers' *physics conclusions* and the JH/Vogel corpus — reported, not endorsed).
2. Peer-reviewed papers' *mathematical identities* (e.g., 2×2 eigenvalues, KK mode-sum decomposition, shear-scalar definition) are **Established**; their *physical model claims* (dark-matter viability, mLQC-I as description of the early universe, magnon-BEC interpretation) are **Source claim** — well-supported in their own domains but not verified by this project and never physically equivalent to RGCS bench systems.
3. Source IDs: **LT** = Lee & Tsai 2026 (PRD 114, L011701, DOI 10.1103/tsq1-bhsz); **GAN** = Gan, Graef, Ramos, Vicente, Wang 2025 (arXiv:2510.14021v1, "Quantum Damping of Cosmological Shear"); **KOS** = Koster et al. 2026 (Nature Physics, DOI 10.1038/s41567-026-03373-6); **TEX** = existing RGCS v2 manuscript; **CORE** = `rgcs_v2/core.py` prototype; **WB2** = RGCS v2 workbook dump; **WB3** = pulse-timing calculator v3 dump; **WB5** = v5 JH-audit workbook dump; **SCAD1/SCAD2** = spiral resonator / crystal generator SCAD; **JH** = curated_findings.csv (v1.8 corpus).
4. Page numbers for LT refer to journal pages L011701-1..7; GAN to arXiv pages 1–7; KOS to the article's ordered pages 1–9 (Methods on p.7, Extended Data pp.8–9).
5. Units: frequencies Hz, lengths mm unless stated. "εR" is the resonance level; RGCS's frequency-domain analogue is ε_R^(f).
