**Global rule.** Adapting an equation's *form* never imports its *physics*.

figure of merit) may be quoted as an RGCS result. No effect demonstrated in
a source's medium may be asserted to occur in quartz without an
independent RGCS derivation (classified) and, for any physical claim, an
experiment.

## Cross-cutting exclusions (apply to ALL sources)

1. **No analogy-only physics.** A shared equation form (two-mode coupling,
   transfer matrix, splitting) does not make two systems physically
   equivalent. Any RGCS physical statement needs its own EST/DER derivation
   plus, for HYP, an experiment with a pre-registered failure condition.
2. **No number laundering.** Isolation, IL, bandwidth, Q, and figure-of-
   merit values stay attributed to their source and medium; they are never
   reported as RGCS performance.
3. **No SRC→physics upgrade.** NHT/HAL and any archival JH/Vogel material
   remain Source claims; parallels with modern math never promote them.
4. **No domain crossing without a licensed substitution.** Optics→acoustics,
   atomic→crystal, and neuroscience→physics transfers require an explicit
   row in the Adaptation Matrix; anything else is excluded by default.
