## What RGCS is

RGCS is an **evidence-governed research framework** that translates unusual
source material into **typed, refutable software** whose default output is a
**refusal or a null**. It does not assert that the source material is true.
It asks one question of every claim:

> It appears possible. Can it be **proved, measured, implemented, or honestly refused**?

So far, for **every physical claim**, the answer has been **refused** or
**unmeasured** — and those null results are published as the primary science,
not hidden. See [../../SCIENTIFIC_BOUNDARIES.md](../../SCIENTIFIC_BOUNDARIES.md)
for the enforced list of what has *not* been established.

## The one thing to remember

A green test suite proves the **software** does what it says. It proves
**nothing physical**. No coil has been wound; no crystal has been driven; no
field has been mapped. Every physical claim remains refused or unmeasured.
